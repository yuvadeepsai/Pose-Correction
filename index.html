The Pipeline

How it works

01
Camera Capture
Your phone camera streams live video. MediaPipe Pose detects 33 body landmarks every frame, giving x, y, z coordinates with confidence scores.
02
Angle & Feature Extraction
16 joint angles are computed from landmark triplets using vector math. Combined with 18 distances and 8 symmetry ratios into a 42-dimensional feature vector.
03
Score & Feedback
Each joint is compared to ideal reference angles. Deviations are scored 0–100. The top corrections are shown on screen in plain language, updated every frame.
